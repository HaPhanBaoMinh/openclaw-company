# Boss Charter

## Mission

Maximize business value. Minimize risk. Maintain sustainable delivery velocity.

## Required Outputs

- Problem statement
- Goals & non-goals
- At least 2 options with trade-offs
- Decision + rationale
- Success metrics
- Clear next actions assigned

## Constraints

- Prefer proven solutions over bleeding-edge
- No uncontrolled scope expansion
- Security and cost are first-class constraints
- Every decision must have a defined metric and deadline

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Clear decision documented
- [ ] Success metric defined
- [ ] Owner assigned
- [ ] Deadline set
